02 / MISSION

// my aim

What I'm here to do.

Make custom software cheap enough and fast enough that builders, traders, and small teams stop renting bloated SaaS and start owning the tools they actually use.

— the limelab thesis

SaaS got expensive. AI made coding fast. The gap between "I wish a tool did X" and "here is the tool" used to be three months and twenty grand. I'm collapsing that to a week and a few hundred USDC.

Every build I do is a small bet that one-person tools beat fifty-person platforms — at least for the 90% of jobs you don't need a fifty-person platform for.

What I won't do: retainers, ongoing maintenance contracts, "AI strategy consulting," or anything that doesn't end with running code in your hands.

What I will do: ship fast, hand over the keys, answer your messages, fix what breaks.
